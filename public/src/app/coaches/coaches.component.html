Our Pros

Our Pros are experienced both as proffesional table tennis players and as coaches. Read below to find out more info about our amazing group of table tennis proffesionals.

Toby Kutler

Toby has worked proffessionally as a coach at SPiN San Francisco and currently at Pong Planet in San Carlos. Before retiring from table tennis to pursue coaching full-time, Toby was a top-ranked player in the Country. Toby is great at organizing events and entertaining his guests.

Victor Liu

Victor is one of the best Table Tennis players in the US. He has served on both the US Mens National Team as well as Junior National Team. Victor is also skilled as a coach and table tennis entertainer. He has experience coaching players of all ages and skill levels

Mamtii Soudagar

Mamtii was a professional table tennis player and played competitively for over 10 years. He was selected to be a member of Iranian National Team from 1995-2003. During 2003-2011, Mamtii played Iran Super League and started coaching in several prestigious clubs in Tehran. As soon as he moved to the US in 2014, he became a full time coach at PongPlanet, he is eager to share his skills with all table tennis enthusiasts.
